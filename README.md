> Built with **React + Vite** on the front end and **Node.js + Express** on the back end.

## ☕ Why Full-Stack Productivity Manager?
This app transforms your workday into focused sprints with **built-in coffee tracking**.  
Log productive sessions, see your brewing habits, and stay motivated.

### Key Features
- 🎯 **Pomodoro Sessions** – Work 25 min, Short 5 min, Long 15 min
- 🔔 **Real-Time Alerts** – Audio notifications on session completion
- 📊 **Session & Coffee Analytics** – Track productivity and coffee brews
- 🌐 **REST API** – Seamless data exchange between client and server
- 💡 **Full-Stack Architecture** – Clean separation of front end and back end
